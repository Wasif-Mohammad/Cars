BMW M3 GTR

The BMW M3 GTR is a high-performance version of the BMW M3, designed primarily for racing. The GTR features a 4.0L V8 engine, producing around 493 hp. It's known for its lightweight construction and incredible handling, making it a legend in the motorsport world.

Toyota Supra

The Toyota Supra is an iconic sports car, famous for its powerful 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine. It produces around 335 hp in its latest version. The Supra is celebrated for its balanced performance, stylish design, and rich history in both street racing and motorsport.
